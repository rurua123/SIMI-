Overview

SIMI Contracting LLC established in 2006, headquartered in the Emirate of Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

The story of our growth is aligned with and has played a proud part in the rapid emergence of United Arab Emirates economy and commercial sector. Our core business and experience is in the construction and maintenance of large scale Roads and Infrastructure development projects serving clients in both the Private and Public sectors.

We are proud to be at the heart of this vibrant industry by combining global insight with local experience, we can help you address challenges at any stage of your projects life-cycle and offer client centric specialist services. We strive to provide exceptional value to our clients by utilizing professional, trusted and pioneering methods.

We have set our sights on green growth as we continue to expand through the integration of highly skilled international team members, state of the art equipment, sustainable building practices and quality-driven vision to continue enhancing the value we bring to our clients.

SIMI Contracting LLC is part of FAID AL NAAM United Co. Ltd. Group. FAID AL NAAM Group Businesses include: FAID AL NAAM for Road and Transport, FAID AL NAAM for Agricultural & Animal Production, FAID AL NAAM United Factory for Manufacturing & Packing Food Foodstuff, FAID AL NAAM for Carton & Paper Industry, Faid Al Naam General Trading LLC, SIMI Contracting LLC & SIMI Landscape & Irrigation LLC.
